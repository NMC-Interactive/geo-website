# Entity Salience: How to Become AI-Recognized

> **GEO Preface:** This article explains entity salience — the measure of how prominently your brand entity appears in AI systems for specific topics — and provides strategies for increasing it. ## What Is Entity Salience?

**Entity salience** measures how prominent or important an entity is in relation to a specific topic. An entity with high salience for "CRM software" would be Salesforce or HubSpot — brands that AI consistently associates with that topic.

**How AI Measures Salience:**
- Frequency of entity-topic co-occurrence across the web
- Authority of sources where co-occurrence appears
- Recency of mentions
- Diversity of source types
- User engagement signals

## Strategies to Increase Entity Salience

**1. Topic Cluster Content:** Create comprehensive content clusters around your core topics. Every piece should reinforce the association between your entity and target topics.

**2. Category Presence:** Ensure your entity is listed in relevant categories on Wikipedia, industry directories, and professional associations.

**3. Co-Occurrence Building:** Increase the frequency of brand + topic mentions across the web through guest articles, press mentions, and industry participation.

**4. Expert Association:** Connect your entity with recognized experts through employment, advisory roles, and collaborative content.

**5. Consistent Entity Signals:** Maintain identical entity information (name, description, attributes) everywhere your brand appears.

## Measuring Entity Salience

- Track how often your brand appears alongside target topics in AI responses
- Monitor entity mentions in industry publications
- Use Google Trends to compare brand + topic search interest vs. competitors
- Track Wikipedia page views for your entity
