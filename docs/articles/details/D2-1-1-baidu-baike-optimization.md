## Why Baidu Baike Is Critical

Baidu Baike is treated as a **primary source of truth** by Baidu's AI (Ernie Bot) and other Chinese AI platforms. A well-crafted Baike entry:
- Establishes your brand as a recognized entity in China
- Provides authoritative information that AI cites directly
- Appears prominently in Baidu search results
- Creates a foundation for all other China GEO activities

## Creating Your Baike Entry

### Eligibility Requirements
- A registered Chinese business entity or local partner
- Verifiable third-party sources about your brand
- Some level of market recognition in China

### Required Content Sections
1. **Basic Information:** Company name, founding date, industry, website
2. **Company History:** Founding story, key milestones, growth timeline
3. **Business Overview:** Products, services, target market, business model
4. **Leadership:** Key executives and their backgrounds
5. **Corporate Culture:** Mission, vision, values
6. **Awards and Recognition:** Industry awards, media mentions
7. **External Links:** Official website, social media, related entries

### Writing Guidelines
- Use neutral, encyclopedic tone (not promotional)
- Cite all claims with verifiable third-party sources
- Write in formal Simplified Chinese
- Include high-quality images with proper licensing
- Keep content updated with current information

### Sources Baike Accepts
- Major Chinese news outlets (Xinhua, 21st Century Business Herald)
- Industry publications (36Kr, PingWest)
- Government registrations
- Academic and research institutions
- Verified official announcements

## Optimization Tips

- Cross-link to related Baike entries
- Add interwiki links where relevant
- Include both Chinese and English names
- Upload company logo and office photos
- Monitor and update quarterly
- Respond to editor feedback promptly
