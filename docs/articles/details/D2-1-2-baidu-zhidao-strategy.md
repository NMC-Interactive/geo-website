## What Is Baidu Zhidao?

Baidu Zhidao (百度知道) is China's largest Q&A platform, similar to Yahoo Answers but with stronger integration into Baidu's search ecosystem. Answers on Zhidao are frequently cited by Baidu's AI and appear in search results.

## Strategy for GEO

**Question Selection:**
- Search for unanswered questions in your industry
- Target questions with high views but few quality answers
- Focus on questions where your expertise provides unique value

**Answer Quality Standards:**
- Write detailed answers (300+ words)
- Use clear structure with headings and bullet points
- Include links to relevant resources on your website
- Add images and diagrams where helpful
- Cite sources to build credibility

**Consistency:**
- Answer 5-10 questions per week
- Maintain a regular schedule
- Build reputation as a knowledgeable contributor
- Engage with follow-up questions

**Zhidao WenDa (知乎问答):**
The professional version of Zhidao offers higher authority signals. Focus on building presence here for B2B GEO.
