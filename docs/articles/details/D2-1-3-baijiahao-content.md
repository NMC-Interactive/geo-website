## Why Baijiahao Matters

Baijiahao is Baidu's native content platform. Articles published here:
- Receive preferential ranking in Baidu search results
- Are frequently cited by Baidu's AI (Ernie Bot)
- Appear in Baidu's content recommendations
- Can drive significant traffic to your website

## Content Strategy

**Publishing Cadence:** 2-4 articles per week

**Article Best Practices:**
- 1,500+ words for in-depth coverage
- Clear headings and structured formatting
- Original images and infographics
- Optimized titles for question-based queries
- Engagement with comments

**Content Types:**
- Industry trend analysis
- How-to guides
- Case studies with Chinese clients
- Expert commentary on industry news
- Data-driven research reports

**Title Optimization:**
- Use question formats ("如何...", "什么是...")
- Include target keywords near the beginning
- Keep titles under 30 Chinese characters
- Make titles specific and compelling
