## Why Douyin Content Matters for Doubao

Doubao is deeply integrated with Douyin. Content that performs well on Douyin:
- Is more likely to be discovered and cited by Doubao
- Generates brand signals that strengthen AI authority
- Creates a content flywheel (Douyin content → Doubao citations → more Douyin followers)

## Content Strategy

**Video Formats That Work:**
- Educational content (how-to, explainers)
- Product demonstrations
- Behind-the-scenes content
- Industry trend commentary
- Customer testimonials

**Optimization Tactics:**
- Post 1-3 videos daily
- Use trending sounds and effects
- Include text overlays (Doubao reads these)
- Use relevant hashtags (research trends)
- Engage with comments promptly
- Go live regularly

**Hashtag Strategy:**
- Research trending hashtags with Douyin's discovery feature
- Create branded campaign hashtags
- Use 3-5 hashtags per video
- Mix broad and niche tags
