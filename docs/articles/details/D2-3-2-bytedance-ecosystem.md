## The ByteDance App Ecosystem

| App | Users | Doubao Integration | GEO Opportunity |
|-----|-------|-------------------|-----------------|
| **Douyin** | 700M+ DAU | Content discovery, search | Video content optimization |
| **CapCut (Jianying)** | 500M+ | Scriptwriting, captions | Creator tool integration |
| **Feishu (Lark)** | 10M+ orgs | Work assistant, document AI | B2B content and tools |
| **Toutiao** | 300M+ | News recommendations | Article publishing |
| **Xigua Video** | 100M+ | Content discovery | Mid-form video |

## Optimization Strategy by Platform

**Douyin:** Video-first content, trending topics, hashtag optimization
**CapCut:** Creator tools, templates, educational content
**Feishu:** B2B tools, enterprise content, professional services
**Toutiao:** Long-form articles, news commentary, industry analysis
**Xigua:** Mid-form educational content, tutorials

## Cross-Platform Content Strategy

Create content that flows across the ecosystem:
1. Publish article on Toutiao
2. Create video summary for Douyin
3. Develop template/tool for CapCut
4. Share in Feishu communities
5. Cross-promote across all platforms
