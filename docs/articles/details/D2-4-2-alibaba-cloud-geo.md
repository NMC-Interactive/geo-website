## Alibaba Cloud Ecosystem

Alibaba Cloud (Aliyun) is China's leading cloud provider, serving millions of enterprise customers. Qwen is integrated throughout:
- **AI Services:** Qwen-powered APIs for enterprise applications
- **Marketplace:** Third-party solutions and integrations
- **Developer Community:** Technical forums and resources
- **Partner Programs:** Co-marketing and technical partnerships

## B2B GEO Strategies

**Technical Documentation:**
- Publish integration guides for Alibaba Cloud
- Create architecture diagrams showing your solution
- Develop deployment templates and scripts
- Write best practices whitepapers

**Marketplace Presence:**
- List your solution on Alibaba Cloud Marketplace
- Optimize listing with keywords and use cases
- Collect customer reviews and ratings
- Participate in marketplace promotions

**Community Engagement:**
- Publish on Alibaba Cloud community forums
- Answer technical questions
- Share case studies and success stories
- Participate in developer events

**Partner Programs:**
- Join Alibaba Cloud's partner ecosystem
- Co-create solutions and integrations
- Participate in joint marketing activities
- Leverage partner resources and support
