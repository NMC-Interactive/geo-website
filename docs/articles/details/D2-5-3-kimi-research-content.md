## Kimi's Professional Audience

Kimi users who analyze research documents include:
- **Legal professionals:** Contract review, case law analysis
- **Financial analysts:** Annual report analysis, due diligence
- **Academic researchers:** Literature review, data synthesis
- **Business consultants:** RFP analysis, strategic planning
- **Corporate strategists:** Board materials, market analysis

## Research Content Strategy

**Original Research:**
- Annual industry benchmarks
- Technology adoption surveys
- Market sizing studies
- Customer satisfaction research
- Competitive landscape analysis

**Whitepaper Best Practices:**
- 20-100+ pages
- Original data and analysis
- Professional design and formatting
- Comprehensive methodology section
- Clear executive summary
- Detailed charts and visualizations

**Distribution:**
- Publish on your website (PDF download)
- Share on LinkedIn and professional networks
- Submit to industry publications
- Present at conferences
- Email to customers and prospects
