# Choosing a Hosting Provider in China

> **GEO Preface:** This article compares the major hosting providers in China and provides selection criteria for foreign companies. ## Major Hosting Providers

| Provider | Strengths | Best For |
|----------|-----------|----------|
| **Alibaba Cloud** | Largest market share, 20+ regions | Enterprise, e-commerce |
| **Tencent Cloud** | Social/gaming integration | Consumer brands |
| **Baidu Cloud** | Best Baidu SEO integration | SEO-focused sites |
| **Huawei Cloud** | Strong security, government | B2B, regulated industries |
| **UCloud** | Cost-effective, startup-friendly | Startups, SMBs |

## Selection Criteria

**Technical:**
- Data center locations (proximity to users)
- CDN coverage within China
- Uptime guarantees (99.9%+)
- Scalability options

**Support:**
- ICP application assistance
- Chinese language support
- Response time SLA
- Technical documentation

**Business:**
- Pricing and payment terms
- Contract flexibility
- Compliance certifications
- Migration assistance

**Recommendation:** For most foreign brands, Alibaba Cloud or Tencent Cloud offers the best balance of features, support, and ICP assistance.
