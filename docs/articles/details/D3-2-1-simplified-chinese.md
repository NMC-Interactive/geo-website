# Simplified Chinese: Beyond Translation

## Simplified vs. Traditional Chinese

| Aspect | Simplified Chinese | Traditional Chinese |
|--------|-------------------|---------------------|
| **Used in** | Mainland China, Singapore | Hong Kong, Macau, Taiwan |
| **Characters** | Fewer strokes, simplified forms | Original complex forms |
| **Example** | 优化 | 優化 |
| **For GEO** | Required for mainland China | Use only for HK/Taiwan markets |

## Why Using the Right Script Matters

- Baidu and Chinese AI platforms index Simplified Chinese
- Using Traditional Chinese for mainland China signals foreignness
- Wrong script can confuse users and reduce engagement
- Some characters differ significantly in meaning between scripts

## Best Practices

- Use Simplified Chinese (简化字) for all mainland China content
- Maintain separate versions for Hong Kong/Taiwan (Traditional)
- Use hreflang tags to differentiate
- Hire native Simplified Chinese writers
- Test rendering on Chinese devices and browsers
