## The Zhihu Authority System

Zhihu authority is built through consistent, high-quality contributions that demonstrate expertise. The platform's algorithm and AI citation systems favor users with:
- High-quality answers with substantial engagement
- Consistent posting across relevant topics
- Professional credentials and verified profiles
- Original insights and data-driven responses

## Step-by-Step Authority Building

**Month 1-2: Foundation**
- Complete profile with professional photo, bio, and credentials
- Identify 10-15 relevant topics in your industry
- Follow industry influencers and engage with their content
- Answer 2-3 questions per week with detailed responses

**Month 3-4: Acceleration**
- Increase to 5-7 answers per week
- Start publishing standalone articles (1-2 per month)
- Create content around trending industry questions
- Build relationships with other contributors

**Month 5-6: Establishment**
- Consistently answer questions in your niche
- Develop signature content series
- Engage with comments and follow-up questions
- Track metrics and optimize approach

**Ongoing: Maintenance**
- Maintain 3-5 answers per week
- Publish 2-4 articles per month
- Update high-performing answers with new information
- Expand into adjacent topics
