## High-Performance Content Formats

**1. Detailed How-To Answers (500-2,000 words)**
- Step-by-step instructions
- Screenshots and diagrams
- Common mistakes to avoid
- Why it gets cited: Provides complete, actionable information

**2. Data-Driven Analysis**
- Original research and statistics
- Charts and visualizations
- Evidence-based conclusions
- Why it gets cited: Unique data AI cannot find elsewhere

**3. Comparison Articles**
- Side-by-side feature comparison
- Pros and cons tables
- Use case recommendations
- Why it gets cited: Helps users make decisions

**4. Expert Explanations**
- Complex topics made accessible
- Industry insider perspective
- Real-world examples
- Why it gets cited: Demonstrates expertise AI trusts

**5. Case Studies**
- Real implementation stories
- Quantified results
- Lessons learned
- Why it gets cited: Provides concrete evidence

## Formatting Best Practices

- Use clear H2/H3 headings
- Include bullet points and numbered lists
- Add images every 300-500 words
- Bold key conclusions
- Keep paragraphs short (3-4 sentences)
- End with a summary
