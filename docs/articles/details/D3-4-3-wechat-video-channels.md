## What Are WeChat Video Channels?

WeChat Video Channels (视频号) is WeChat's short-video platform, integrated directly into the WeChat app. It combines elements of Douyin and Instagram Reels with WeChat's social graph.

## Why Video Channels Matter for GEO

- Video content is indexed and discoverable within WeChat Search
- Videos can be shared in chats and Moments
- Live streaming generates real-time engagement
- Video content can be cited by AI assistants
- Vertical video format is mobile-optimized

## Content Strategy

**Video Types:**
- Product demonstrations (1-3 minutes)
- Expert interviews and thought leadership
- Behind-the-scenes content
- Event coverage and webinar recordings
- Customer testimonials

**Best Practices:**
- Post 3-5 videos per week
- Use vertical format (9:16)
- Include captions (many watch without sound)
- Engage with comments
- Cross-promote with Public Account articles

**Live Streaming:**
- Host product launches and announcements
- Conduct Q&A sessions
- Present at virtual events
- Interview industry experts
