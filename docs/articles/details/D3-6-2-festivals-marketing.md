## Key Marketing Dates

| Festival | Date | Theme | Content Opportunity |
|----------|------|-------|---------------------|
| **Chinese New Year** | Jan/Feb | Family, reunion, gifts | Family-oriented content, red envelope campaigns |
| **Valentine's Day** | Feb 14 | Romance, couples | Couples marketing, gift guides |
| **Women's Day** | Mar 8 | Female empowerment | Women in leadership, product campaigns |
| **618 Festival** | Jun 18 | Shopping, deals | Major e-commerce push, product launches |
| **Qixi Festival** | Aug | Romance (Chinese Valentine's) | Traditional romance themes |
| **Mid-Autumn Festival** | Sep/Oct | Family, reunion | Mooncake-themed content, family messaging |
| **Singles' Day (11.11)** | Nov 11 | Shopping, biggest event | Largest campaign of the year |
| **Double 12** | Dec 12 | Secondary shopping | Clearance and follow-up campaigns |

## GEO Optimization for Festivals

- Create seasonal content 2-3 months in advance
- Use festival-specific keywords (search volume spikes)
- AI platforms prioritize timely, relevant content
- Plan content calendars around these dates
- Create evergreen festival content that can be updated annually
