## Content Strategy Framework

**Posting Cadence:**
- Company page: 1-2 posts per day
- Executive profiles: 3-5 posts per week
- Articles: 1-2 per month

**Content Mix:**
- 40% Educational (industry insights, how-to, trends)
- 30% Company news (products, partnerships, milestones)
- 20% Thought leadership (opinions, predictions, analysis)
- 10% Engagement (polls, questions, community)

**Post Best Practices:**
- Include images or videos (2x engagement)
- Write compelling first lines
- Use 3-5 relevant hashtags
- Tag relevant people and companies
- Post during Western business hours
- Respond to all comments

**Article Topics:**
- Industry trend predictions
- Lessons from the Chinese market
- Technology innovation stories
- Global expansion insights
- Leadership and management

**Content Calendar:**
- Monday: Industry news commentary
- Tuesday: Educational content
- Wednesday: Company update
- Thursday: Thought leadership
- Friday: Community engagement
