## LinkedIn Ad Types

**Sponsored Content:**
- Promote posts to targeted audiences
- Best for: Brand awareness, content promotion
- Format: Single image, carousel, video, document

**Sponsored Messaging:**
- Send direct messages to prospects
- Best for: Lead generation, event invitations
- Format: Conversation ads, message ads

**Lead Gen Forms:**
- Collect leads within LinkedIn
- Best for: Demo requests, whitepaper downloads
- Pre-filled forms reduce friction

**Dynamic Ads:**
- Personalized ads based on profile data
- Best for: Follower acquisition, job postings
- Format: Follower ads, spotlight ads, job ads

## Targeting for Chinese Brands

**Job Titles:** Target decision-makers (C-level, VP, Director)
**Industries:** Focus on industries where you have case studies
**Company Size:** Match to your ideal customer profile
**Geography:** Start with 2-3 priority markets
**Skills:** Target users with relevant technical skills

**Budget Guidelines:**
- LinkedIn CPM: $30-50 (higher than other platforms)
- LinkedIn CPC: $5-15
- Minimum daily budget: $10-20
- Focus on high-value audiences, not broad reach

## Campaign Best Practices

- Test organic content before promoting
- Use lead gen forms for direct response
- A/B test headlines and images
- Retarget website visitors
- Measure quality of leads, not just quantity
