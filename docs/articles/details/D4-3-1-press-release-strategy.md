## Press Release Strategy

**Newsworthy Topics:**
- Product launches with unique technology
- Funding rounds and financial milestones
- Strategic partnerships with known brands
- Executive appointments
- Major customer wins with quantified results
- Original research and data

**Press Release Structure:**
1. **Headline:** Clear, specific, and newsworthy
2. **Dateline:** Location and date
3. **Lead Paragraph:** Who, what, when, where, why
4. **Body:** Details, quotes, supporting information
5. **Boilerplate:** Company description
6. **Contact:** Media contact information

**Distribution Strategy:**
- **Wire services:** PR Newswire, Business Wire, GlobeNewswire
- **Direct pitching:** Target specific journalists
- **Embargo strategy:** Offer exclusive early access
- **Follow-up:** Track opens and follow up personally

**Best Practices:**
- Write in native English (not translated)
- Include executive quotes
- Add multimedia (images, videos, infographics)
- Optimize for SEO with relevant keywords
- Include clear call-to-action
