## Building Media Relationships

**Step 1: Research**
- Identify 20-50 target journalists in your industry
- Read their recent articles to understand their interests
- Follow them on Twitter/X and engage with their content
- Use tools like Muck Rack or Cision

**Step 2: Initial Outreach**
- Introduce yourself without pitching
- Offer value (data, insights, access)
- Be respectful of their time
- Personalize every outreach

**Step 3: Nurture**
- Share relevant industry news
- Offer commentary on trending topics
- Invite to events and briefings
- Maintain regular but not excessive contact

**Step 4: Leverage**
- Offer exclusive stories
- Provide early access to announcements
- Make executives available for interviews
- Share customer stories and data

## Key Principles

- **Be helpful, not pushy:** Journalists value sources who make their job easier
- **Respect deadlines:** Respond quickly to inquiries
- **Be honest:** Never mislead or overpromise
- **Think long-term:** Relationships matter more than single placements
