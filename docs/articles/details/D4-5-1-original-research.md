## Why Original Research Is the Ultimate GEO Asset

Original research provides **unique data** that AI models cannot find anywhere else. When your research contains proprietary statistics, findings, or benchmarks, AI must cite you as the source every time that data is relevant.

## Types of Original Research

**Industry Benchmarks:**
- Annual surveys of technology adoption
- Salary and compensation reports
- Market sizing and forecast studies

**Technology Assessments:**
- Performance benchmarking
- Security and compliance evaluations
- Cost comparison studies

**Consumer Research:**
- Buying behavior surveys
- Satisfaction and loyalty studies
- Preference and trend research

## Research Process

**Step 1: Define Research Question**
- Identify a question your audience cares about
- Ensure you can access the data needed
- Confirm the research hasn't been done recently

**Step 2: Design Methodology**
- Choose quantitative, qualitative, or mixed methods
- Define sample size and selection criteria
- Create survey instruments or interview guides

**Step 3: Collect Data**
- Survey your customers and prospects
- Analyze proprietary usage data
- Conduct expert interviews
- Review secondary sources

**Step 4: Analyze and Visualize**
- Use statistical analysis where appropriate
- Create clear, compelling charts
- Identify key findings and trends

**Step 5: Publish and Distribute**
- Write comprehensive report (20-50+ pages)
- Create executive summary for social media
- Pitch to media and industry publications
- Present at conferences
