## Types of Data-Driven Content

**Benchmark Reports:**
- Annual industry performance benchmarks
- Technology adoption rates by company size
- Regional comparison studies

**Trend Reports:**
- Emerging technology trends
- Market growth projections
- Consumer behavior shifts

**Statistical Content:**
- Infographics with original data
- Data visualization articles
- Interactive dashboards

**Survey Results:**
- Customer satisfaction surveys
- Employee engagement studies
- Industry outlook surveys

## Creating Data-Driven Content

**Data Sources:**
- Proprietary customer data (anonymized)
- Public datasets (government, academic)
- Survey responses
- Third-party data partnerships

**Visualization Best Practices:**
- Use clear, labeled charts
- Choose appropriate chart types
- Include data source citations
- Make charts shareable (embed codes)

**Distribution:**
- Publish full report as PDF
- Create blog post summaries
- Share key findings on social media
- Pitch data stories to journalists
