## Why WeChat Is Essential for B2B GEO

WeChat (微信) is often misunderstood by Western marketers as "China's WhatsApp" or "China's Facebook." It is neither. WeChat is a **super-app** — a platform that integrates messaging, social media, content publishing, e-commerce, payments, and enterprise tools into a single ecosystem. For B2B GEO in China, WeChat is not optional; it is indispensable.

**Key Statistics:**
- **1.3 billion** monthly active users
- **900 million** daily active users
- Average user opens WeChat **30+ times per day**
- **45 billion** messages sent daily
- Over **4 million** WeChat Public Accounts (official accounts)

### Why WeChat Content Is Powerful for GEO

**1. Permanent Indexing:** Unlike social media posts on Western platforms that disappear in days, WeChat articles are permanent web pages with their own URLs. They are indexed by Baidu and other search engines, meaning a well-written WeChat article can drive traffic and citations for years.

**2. AI Citation Frequency:** Chinese AI platforms — including DeepSeek, Baidu ERNIE, and even Doubao — frequently cite WeChat Public Account articles as authoritative sources. A comprehensive WeChat article on a technical topic is highly likely to be referenced by AI when answering related questions.

**3. Professional Audience:** WeChat is used by virtually every professional in China. Business decisions, vendor research, and industry discussions happen on WeChat. For B2B brands, this is where your audience lives.

**4. Ecosystem Integration:** WeChat content can be shared in group chats, posted on Moments (the social feed), embedded in Mini Programs, and discovered through WeChat Search. This creates multiple distribution channels from a single content asset.

## WeChat Public Accounts (公众号)

### Types of Public Accounts

**Subscription Accounts (订阅号):**
- Best for content-heavy brands
- Can publish 1 article per day
- Content appears in the user's "Subscriptions" folder
- Lower visibility but higher publishing frequency
- Best for: Media, thought leadership, content marketing

**Service Accounts (服务号):**
- Best for brands focused on customer service and conversions
- Can publish 4 articles per month
- Content appears directly in the user's chat list (higher visibility)
- Access to advanced APIs (payments, customer service, templates)
- Best for: E-commerce, SaaS, brands with strong customer interaction

### Setting Up Your Public Account

**Requirements:**
- A Chinese business license (or foreign business license for overseas accounts)
- A Chinese ID for the account administrator
- A verified email address and phone number
- A brand name (can be in English or Chinese)

**Verification Process:**
1. Register on the WeChat Public Platform (mp.weixin.qq.com)
2. Submit business documentation
3. Pay verification fee (300 RMB annually)
4. Complete verification (typically 3-7 business days)

### Content Strategy for GEO

**Article Best Practices:**
- **Length:** 1,500-5,000 words for in-depth articles (longer than typical social media)
- **Format:** Use WeChat's native formatting tools — headings, bullet points, images, and embedded videos
- **Headlines:** Write compelling headlines that work for both human readers and AI extraction
- **Structure:** Begin with a clear summary, use descriptive headings, and end with a strong conclusion
- **Visuals:** Include original images, infographics, and charts every 300-500 words
- **CTA:** End with a clear call-to-action (follow the account, download a resource, contact sales)

**Content Types That Drive GEO:**
- Industry trend reports and analysis
- How-to guides and tutorials
- Product comparisons and evaluations
- Case studies with Chinese clients
- Expert interviews and roundups
- Original research and data reports
- Regulatory analysis and compliance guides

**Publishing Cadence:**
- Minimum: 2 articles per week
- Optimal: 3-4 articles per week
- Quality matters more than quantity — one excellent article beats three mediocre ones

### Optimization for AI Citations

**Structure for Extractability:**
- Use clear H2 and H3 headings that reflect common questions
- Include definitional paragraphs at the start of sections
- Add FAQ sections at the end of articles
- Use comparison tables for product/service evaluations
- Include numbered lists for step-by-step processes

**SEO Elements:**
- Write a compelling article summary (appears in search results)
- Use relevant keywords in the title and headings
- Add tags to articles for categorization
- Cross-link to other articles in your account

## WeChat Mini Programs (小程序)

### What Are Mini Programs?

Mini Programs are lightweight apps that run within WeChat, requiring no download or installation. They can be accessed through search, QR codes, embedded in articles, or shared in chats. With over 400 million daily active users, Mini Programs represent a massive opportunity for B2B engagement.

### Mini Programs for B2B GEO

**Types of Mini Programs:**
- **Product Configurators:** Let prospects configure and price your solutions
- **ROI Calculators:** Help prospects calculate return on investment
- **Assessment Tools:** Diagnostic quizzes that recommend solutions
- **Resource Libraries:** Searchable repositories of whitepapers, case studies, and videos
- **Event Registration:** Registration and management for webinars and events
- **Customer Portals:** Account access for existing customers

**GEO Benefits:**
- Mini Programs are indexed by WeChat Search and can appear in AI responses
- Interactive tools generate engagement signals that strengthen brand authority
- When Doubao or other AI assistants recommend tools, well-designed Mini Programs can be featured
- QR codes for Mini Programs can be included in offline marketing materials

### Mini Program Development

**Options:**
1. **Custom Development:** Build from scratch using WeChat's development framework (highest flexibility, highest cost)
2. **Low-Code Platforms:** Use WeChat's low-code tools or third-party platforms (faster, lower cost)
3. **Third-Party Solutions:** Purchase pre-built Mini Program templates (fastest, least customizable)

**Development Considerations:**
- Must comply with WeChat's content and functionality guidelines
- Requires a Chinese business entity for publishing
- Subject to WeChat's review process before going live
- Must be maintained and updated regularly

## WeChat Search Optimization

WeChat has its own search function that indexes content within the WeChat ecosystem, including Public Account articles, Mini Programs, video content, and conversations.

**Optimization Tactics:**
- Use relevant keywords in article titles and content
- Register your brand in WeChat Search
- Encourage users to search for your brand on WeChat
- Create content around trending topics in WeChat Search
- Optimize Mini Program names and descriptions for search

## Integration with Other Platforms

**WeChat + Baidu:** WeChat articles are indexed by Baidu, so WeChat content directly contributes to Baidu SEO and GEO.

**WeChat + Zhihu:** Share Zhihu answers in WeChat articles and vice versa for cross-platform authority building.

**WeChat + Offline:** Use QR codes on business cards, brochures, and at events to drive WeChat follows and Mini Program usage.

## Measuring WeChat GEO Performance

- **Article Metrics:** Reads, shares, likes, comments, and completion rate
- **Follower Growth:** Net new followers per week/month
- **Mini Program Usage:** Daily active users, session duration, conversion actions
- **Search Visibility:** Appearance in WeChat Search results
- **AI Citation Rate:** How often your WeChat content is cited by Chinese AI
- **Referral Traffic:** Website visits from WeChat articles and Mini Programs
- **Lead Generation:** Form fills, demo requests, and sales inquiries from WeChat
