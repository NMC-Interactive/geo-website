## Kimi's 2-Million-Character Context Window

Kimi's defining feature is its ability to process up to **2 million Chinese characters** (approximately 1 million English words) in a single session. This is roughly equivalent to:
- 20 novels
- 100 research papers
- An entire legal case file
- A complete corporate annual report suite

## Content Strategy Implications

**What This Enables:**
- Analysis of entire books, reports, and document collections
- Multi-document comparison and synthesis
- Deep research assistance across large corpora
- Complex data extraction from long documents

**What Content Creators Should Do:**
- Publish comprehensive, long-form documents
- Create detailed research reports (50+ pages)
- Include extensive appendices with supporting data
- Structure documents with clear navigation (TOC, bookmarks)
- Write detailed executive summaries at the beginning

**Document Structure Best Practices:**
- Table of contents with clickable links
- Executive summary (1-2 pages)
- Clear section hierarchy (H1, H2, H3)
- Data tables and comparison matrices
- Detailed appendices
- Glossary of terms
