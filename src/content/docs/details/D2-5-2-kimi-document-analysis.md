## Document Formats Kimi Processes

Kimi can analyze:
- PDF documents (text-based, not scanned images)
- Word documents (.doc, .docx)
- Text files (.txt, .md)
- Web pages (via URL)
- Multiple documents simultaneously

## PDF Optimization for Kimi

**Critical Requirements:**
- Text must be selectable (not image-based)
- Use proper heading tags in source documents
- Include document metadata (title, author, subject)
- Embed fonts for consistent rendering
- Add bookmarks for documents over 20 pages
- Optimize file size (<50MB for upload)

**Structure Best Practices:**
- Descriptive headings at all levels
- Executive summary at the beginning
- Key findings highlighted in callout boxes
- Comprehensive table of contents
- Numbered sections and subsections
- Clear data tables with headers

## Content Types That Perform Well

- Annual industry reports
- Legal and compliance guides
- Technical whitepapers
- Market research studies
- Competitive analysis reports
- Corporate sustainability reports
