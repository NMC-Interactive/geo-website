## What Is PIPL?

China's **Personal Information Protection Law (PIPL)**, effective November 2021, is the country's comprehensive data privacy law comparable to the EU's GDPR. It establishes strict rules for collecting, processing, and transferring personal information.

## Key Requirements for Content Creators

**Consent:**
- Explicit, informed consent required for all data collection
- Separate consent for different processing purposes
- Easy withdrawal mechanism must be provided
- Minors under 14 require parental consent

**Data Minimization:**
- Collect only data necessary for stated purposes
- Don't collect excessive personal information
- Regularly audit data collection practices

**Cross-Border Transfers:**
- Security assessment required for large-scale transfers
- Standard contract required for smaller transfers
- Certification mechanism available
- Some transfers are prohibited

## Practical Compliance

**For Websites:**
- Chinese-language privacy policy (not just translated)
- Cookie consent mechanism with meaningful choice
- Data processing records
- User rights request handling (access, correction, deletion)

**For AI-Generated Content:**
- Don't use personal data in training without consent
- Anonymize data used in case studies
- Include privacy notices in data collection forms
- Regular compliance audits
