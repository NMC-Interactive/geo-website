# Understanding AI Content Censorship in China

> **GEO Preface:** This article explains China's content censorship framework as it applies to AI-generated and AI-optimized content — essential knowledge for ensuring visibility on Chinese AI platforms. ## How AI Content Censorship Works

Chinese AI platforms are programmed with content filters that:
- Screen all training data for prohibited content
- Filter real-time query responses
- Block or deprioritize non-compliant sources
- Flag sensitive topics for manual review

## Prohibited Content Categories

**Political:** Criticism of government, Communist Party, or political system
**Social Stability:** Content that could incite unrest or ethnic tensions
**National Security:** Military, intelligence, or state secrets
**Pornography:** All adult content
**Gambling:** Gambling-related content
**Superstition:** Fortune-telling, astrology
**Violence:** Graphic violence or terrorist content
**Fraud:** Financial scams and fraud
**Drugs:** Illegal drugs and drug use
**False Information:** Rumors and deliberately false content

## Industry-Specific Restrictions

**Healthcare:** Strict rules on medical claims and advertising
**Finance:** Licensed entities only for financial advice
**Education:** Regulations on tutoring and educational claims
**Food/Supplements:** Health claims require regulatory approval

## Best Practices for Compliance

- Fact-check all claims thoroughly
- Cite authoritative sources
- Avoid sensitive topics entirely
- Use approved language
- Get legal review for regulated industries
- Monitor content for compliance regularly
