# ICP Application Timeline & Requirements

> **GEO Preface:** This article provides a detailed timeline and requirements checklist for the ICP application process. This detail article is part of our [ICP License Guide](/world-to-china/icp-license-guide/) spoke within the [World-to-China GEO](/world-to-china-geo/) pillar. For ICP types, read [ICP Types](/world-to-china/icp-license-guide/icp-types/). For hosting providers, see [Hosting Providers in China](/world-to-china/icp-license-guide/hosting-providers-china/).

---

## ICP Bei An Timeline

| Phase | Duration | Activities |
|-------|----------|------------|
| Preparation | 1-2 weeks | Gather documents, choose hosting |
| Hosting Review | 1-3 days | Provider reviews application |
| Government Review | 10-20 days | Provincial administration review |
| Approval | 1-3 days | ICP number issued |
| **Total** | **2-4 weeks** | |

## ICP Jing Ying Timeline

| Phase | Duration | Activities |
|-------|----------|------------|
| WFOE Setup | 2-3 months | Establish Chinese entity |
| Document Preparation | 2-4 weeks | Business plan, technical solution |
| Application Submission | 1 week | Submit to MIIT |
| Review Process | 1-2 months | Detailed review and possible interviews |
| Approval | 1-2 weeks | License issued |
| **Total** | **3-6 months** | |

## Required Documents

**ICP Bei An:**
- Business license
- Domain certificate
- Hosting agreement
- Responsible person's ID
- Photo of person holding ID
- Security commitment letter

**ICP Jing Ying (additional):**
- Detailed business plan
- Technical solution documentation
- Capital verification
- Employment records
- Office lease agreement
