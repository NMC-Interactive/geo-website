# How to Create a Baidu Baike Entry

> **GEO Preface:** This article provides a step-by-step guide to creating a Baidu Baike entry for foreign brands entering China. This detail article is part of our [Baidu Baike & Zhihu](/world-to-china/baidu-baike-zhihu/) spoke within the [World-to-China GEO](/world-to-china-geo/) pillar. ## Step-by-Step Creation Process

**Step 1: Check for Existing Entry**
Search Baidu Baike for your brand name. If an entry exists, review and plan improvements.

**Step 2: Register Baidu Account**
Create a verified Baidu account at baidu.com.

**Step 3: Prepare Content**
Write neutral, encyclopedic content covering: company history, products/services, leadership, milestones, and awards. Cite all claims.

**Step 4: Gather Sources**
Collect Chinese news articles, government registrations, and verified third-party sources.

**Step 5: Submit Entry**
Navigate to Baike, click "Create Entry," fill in all sections, add citations, and submit for review.

**Step 6: Respond to Feedback**
Baike editors may request changes. Respond promptly with revisions.

**Step 7: Maintain and Update**
Review quarterly. Update with new milestones, products, and leadership changes.
