## Why Public Accounts Matter for B2B

WeChat Public Accounts are the single most important content platform for B2B GEO in China because:
- Articles are permanent and indexed by Baidu
- Content is frequently cited by Chinese AI platforms
- WeChat is used by virtually every professional in China
- Articles can be shared in groups and Moments

## Account Type Selection

**Subscription Account:**
- 1 article per day
- Lower visibility but higher frequency
- Best for: Content-heavy B2B brands

**Service Account:**
- 4 articles per month
- Higher visibility in chat list
- Best for: Brands focused on conversions

## Content Strategy

**Publishing Cadence:** 3-4 articles per week (Subscription) or weekly (Service)

**Article Structure:**
- Compelling headline (15-30 Chinese characters)
- Rich media (images, infographics, videos)
- 1,500-5,000 words
- Clear headings and formatting
- Strong call-to-action

**Content Types:**
- Industry trend reports
- How-to guides
- Case studies
- Expert interviews
- Original research
