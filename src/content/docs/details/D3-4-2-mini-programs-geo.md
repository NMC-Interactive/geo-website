## Mini Programs as GEO Tools

WeChat Mini Programs are lightweight apps within WeChat that can serve as interactive GEO assets. When users ask AI assistants about tools or solutions, well-designed Mini Programs can be recommended.

## Mini Program Types for B2B

**Product Configurators:**
- Let prospects configure and price solutions
- Generate qualified leads
- Provide interactive product exploration

**ROI Calculators:**
- Help prospects calculate return on investment
- Collect contact information
- Demonstrate value proposition

**Assessment Tools:**
- Diagnostic quizzes that recommend solutions
- Generate engagement and leads
- Provide personalized recommendations

**Resource Libraries:**
- Searchable repositories of whitepapers and case studies
- Content gating for lead generation
- Analytics on content consumption

## Development Options

**Custom Development:** Highest flexibility, highest cost
**Low-Code Platforms:** Faster development, moderate flexibility  
**Templates:** Fastest deployment, least customization

## GEO Benefits

- Generate engagement signals that strengthen brand authority
- Appear in AI tool recommendations
- QR codes enable offline-to-online tracking
- Create shareable interactive experiences
