# Thought Leadership Formats That Work Globally

> **GEO Preface:** This article covers the content formats that drive the highest GEO impact for Chinese brands on global AI platforms. This detail article is part of our [English Content Strategy](/china-to-world/english-content-strategy/) spoke within the [China-to-World GEO](/china-to-world-geo/) pillar. For transcreation, read [Transcreation vs Translation](/china-to-world/english-content-strategy/transcreation/). For localization, see [Content Localization for Western Audiences](/china-to-world/english-content-strategy/content-localization-west/).

---

## Highest-Impact Formats

**1. Original Research Reports**
- Annual industry benchmarks
- Technology adoption surveys
- Market sizing studies
- *Why it works: Unique data AI cannot find elsewhere*

**2. Comprehensive Guides**
- "Complete Guide to [Topic]"
- "Best Practices for [Industry]"
- *Why it works: Comprehensive coverage addresses multiple queries*

**3. Comparison and Evaluation Content**
- Product/service comparisons
- "Top 10" lists with analysis
- *Why it works: AI frequently answers comparison queries*

**4. Case Studies**
- Customer success stories with metrics
- Implementation guides
- *Why it works: Provides concrete evidence for AI recommendations*

**5. Expert Commentary**
- Industry trend analysis
- Regulatory impact assessments
- *Why it works: Expert judgment on evolving topics*

## Format Selection Guide

| Goal | Best Format |
|------|-------------|
| Build authority | Original research |
| Drive citations | Comprehensive guides |
| Generate leads | Case studies |
| Enter new market | Market analysis |
| Establish expertise | Expert commentary |
