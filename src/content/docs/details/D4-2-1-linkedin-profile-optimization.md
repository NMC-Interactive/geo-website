## Profile Optimization Checklist

**Profile Photo:**
- Professional headshot with neutral background
- Business attire appropriate for your industry
- Friendly, approachable expression
- High resolution (minimum 400x400 pixels)

**Headline:**
- Include value proposition, not just job title
- Example: "Helping Enterprises Adopt AI | CTO at [Company]"
- Use relevant keywords
- Keep under 220 characters

**About Section:**
- 3-5 paragraphs telling your professional story
- Include achievements with metrics
- Mention your company's mission and value
- End with a call-to-action
- Use first person

**Experience Section:**
- Detailed descriptions with achievements
- Include metrics and quantified results
- Use bullet points for readability
- Highlight international experience

**Featured Section:**
- Pin your best articles and posts
- Include media mentions and interviews
- Showcase presentations and talks
- Link to company resources

**Skills & Endorsements:**
- List 50 skills (maximum allowed)
- Prioritize industry-relevant skills
- Pin top 3 most important skills
- Seek endorsements from colleagues

**Recommendations:**
- Request recommendations from supervisors, clients, and partners
- Offer to write recommendations in return
- Aim for 5-10 high-quality recommendations
