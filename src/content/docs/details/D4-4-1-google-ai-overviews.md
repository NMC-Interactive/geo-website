## What Are AI Overviews?

AI Overviews are AI-generated summaries that appear at the top of Google search results for certain queries. They synthesize information from multiple sources and include citations.

## How AI Overviews Select Content

Google's AI Overviews pull from:
- Featured snippets
- High-ranking authoritative content
- Structured data (FAQ, HowTo)
- Knowledge Graph entities
- Recent, relevant content

## Optimization Strategies

**Featured Snippet Optimization:**
- Include definitional paragraphs (40-60 words)
- Use question-based H2 headings
- Create comparison tables
- Use ordered lists for processes

**E-E-A-T Enhancement:**
- Expert authorship with credentials
- Cite authoritative sources
- Keep content fresh and updated
- Build brand authority signals

**Structured Data:**
- Implement FAQPage schema
- Use HowTo schema for tutorials
- Add Article schema to all content
- Include Review/AggregateRating

**Content Freshness:**
- Regularly update cornerstone content
- Add "Last Updated" dates
- Publish about current trends
- Create annual update posts
