## Understanding Gemini

Google Gemini is a multimodal AI assistant that can process text, images, code, and audio. It is integrated into:
- Google Search (AI Overviews)
- Google Workspace (Docs, Gmail, Sheets)
- Android devices
- The standalone Gemini web app

## Gemini-Specific Optimization

**Multimodal Content:**
- Create video content (Gemini processes YouTube videos)
- Design infographics and data visualizations
- Include high-quality images with descriptive alt text
- Create audio/podcast content with transcripts

**Google Workspace Integration:**
- Content in Google Docs may be processed by Gemini
- Gmail conversations can be summarized
- Google Sheets data can be analyzed
- Ensure your public Google Workspace content is optimized

**YouTube Optimization:**
- Gemini can watch and summarize YouTube videos
- Create detailed video descriptions with timestamps
- Add transcripts and captions
- Use chapters for longer videos
- Include relevant keywords in titles and descriptions

**Google Business Profile:**
- Maintain accurate business information
- Respond to reviews
- Add photos and updates
- This feeds into Gemini's local knowledge

**Technical:**
- Ensure content is accessible to Google's crawlers
- Use proper schema markup
- Maintain fast page speeds
- Implement mobile-first design
