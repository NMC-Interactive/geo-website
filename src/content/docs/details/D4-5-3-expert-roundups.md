## What Is an Expert Roundup?

An expert roundup is a piece of content that collects insights, opinions, or predictions from multiple industry experts on a specific topic. Example: "15 AI Experts Predict the Future of Search in 2026."

## Why Roundups Work for GEO

- **Authority by association:** Your brand is associated with recognized experts
- **Multiple citation targets:** Each expert may share and link to the content
- **Comprehensive coverage:** AI values content that presents multiple perspectives
- **Network effects:** Contributors promote to their audiences

## How to Create an Expert Roundup

**Step 1: Choose a Compelling Topic**
- Timely and relevant to your industry
- Debatable or forward-looking (predictions work well)
- Specific enough to generate interesting answers

**Step 2: Identify and Recruit Experts**
- Industry analysts and consultants
- Executives at complementary companies
- Academics and researchers
- Influencers and thought leaders
- Target 10-20 participants for substantial content

**Step 3: Ask a Clear, Specific Question**
- One question is often enough
- Make it easy to answer (2-3 paragraphs)
- Provide a deadline and format guidance

**Step 4: Compile and Publish**
- Organize responses thematically
- Add your own analysis and commentary
- Include expert photos and bios
- Create shareable quote graphics

**Step 5: Promote**
- Notify all contributors when published
- Create social media posts tagging each expert
- Pitch to industry publications
- Repurpose into additional content formats
