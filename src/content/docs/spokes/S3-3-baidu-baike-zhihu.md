## Why Baike and Zhihu Matter for GEO

In the Chinese AI ecosystem, authority is not built solely on your own website. Chinese AI platforms — Baidu ERNIE, DeepSeek, Qwen, and Doubao — all heavily weight signals from **Baidu Baike** (百度百科) and **Zhihu** (知乎) when evaluating brand credibility and selecting content to cite.

**Baidu Baike** is China's equivalent of Wikipedia. With over 27 million entries and deep integration into Baidu's search and AI systems, a Baike entry for your brand functions as a foundational source of truth. When Chinese AI models need to verify facts about a company, person, or product, they turn to Baike first.

**Zhihu** is China's premier professional question-and-answer platform, often compared to Quora but with a stronger focus on expert, in-depth responses. Zhihu answers are frequently cited by DeepSeek, Qwen, and other AI platforms when answering technical and professional questions. A well-crafted Zhihu presence positions your brand as an industry expert.

Together, these platforms form the cornerstone of authority building for World-to-China GEO.

## Part 1: Baidu Baike Optimization

### Understanding Baidu Baike

Baidu Baike operates similarly to Wikipedia but with some key differences:
- **Editorial Control:** Baike has more active editorial oversight than Wikipedia
- **Commercial Content:** While not overtly promotional, Baike entries can include product information
- **Verification:** Baike offers verified entries for brands and public figures
- **Integration:** Baike content appears directly in Baidu search results and AI responses

### Step 1: Check for Existing Entry

Before creating a new entry, search Baidu Baike for your brand name. If an entry already exists:
- Review it for accuracy and completeness
- Identify missing or incorrect information
- Plan updates to improve quality

If no entry exists, proceed to creation.

### Step 2: Determine Eligibility

Baike has notability requirements similar to Wikipedia:
- Your brand should have significant coverage in Chinese media
- There should be verifiable, third-party sources about your brand
- Your brand should have some level of recognition in China

**For Foreign Brands:**
- International recognition helps but is not sufficient
- Chinese media coverage significantly strengthens eligibility
- Having Chinese clients, partners, or operations helps
- Industry awards and recognition are valuable supporting evidence

### Step 3: Prepare Content

A comprehensive Baike entry should include:

**Basic Information:**
- Company name (Chinese and English)
- Founding date and location
- Industry category
- Key products or services
- Company size/employee count
- Official website

**Company History:**
- Founding story
- Key milestones and achievements
- Major product launches
- Funding rounds (if applicable)
- Expansion milestones

**Business Overview:**
- Detailed description of products/services
- Target market and customer segments
- Business model
- Key differentiators

**Leadership:**
- Founder and key executives
- Brief professional backgrounds

**Corporate Culture:**
- Mission and vision
- Values
- Work environment

**Awards and Recognition:**
- Industry awards
- Media recognition
- Certifications

**External Links:**
- Official website
- Social media profiles
- Related Baike entries

### Step 4: Gather Sources

Baike requires citations from verifiable, third-party sources:
- Chinese news articles about your company
- Industry reports that mention your brand
- Government registration documents
- Verified social media accounts
- Official company announcements (used carefully)

**Source Quality Hierarchy:**
1. Major Chinese news outlets (Xinhua, People's Daily, 21st Century Business Herald)
2. Industry publications (36Kr, PingWest, TechNode Chinese)
3. Government sources (MIIT, SAMR)
4. Academic or research institutions
5. Recognized industry associations

### Step 5: Create the Entry

**Process:**
1. Register a Baidu account
2. Navigate to Baike and click "Create Entry"
3. Fill in all sections with well-sourced, neutral content
4. Add citations for all factual claims
5. Upload relevant images (logo, office photos, product images)
6. Submit for review

**Tips:**
- Write in a neutral, encyclopedic tone (not promotional)
- Use formal, professional Chinese
- Include all available citations
- Ensure images are high-quality and properly licensed
- Be prepared for multiple rounds of editorial review

### Step 6: Maintain and Update

Once your entry is live:
- Review it quarterly for accuracy
- Update with new milestones, products, and achievements
- Monitor for unauthorized edits
- Add new citations as media coverage grows

## Part 2: Zhihu Authority Building

### Understanding Zhihu

Zhihu has over 100 million registered users and is the go-to platform for professional knowledge sharing in China. Its content is particularly valued by:
- **DeepSeek:** Frequently cites Zhihu answers for technical and professional questions
- **Qwen:** Uses Zhihu content for business and technology queries
- **Baidu ERNIE:** Includes Zhihu answers in search results and AI responses

### Strategy 1: Establish Your Profile

**Profile Optimization:**
- Use a professional headshot
- Write a comprehensive bio highlighting your expertise
- Include your company affiliation
- Add credentials and qualifications
- Link to your website and other professional profiles

**For Company Accounts:**
- Verify your company account (企业号)
- Complete all profile fields
- Add team members as contributors

### Strategy 2: Answer Questions Strategically

**Question Selection:**
- Search for questions in your industry using relevant keywords
- Target questions with high follower counts but few quality answers
- Focus on questions where your expertise provides unique value
- Monitor Zhihu's "Recommended Questions" in your field

**Answer Quality Standards:**
- Write comprehensive answers (500-2,000 words)
- Use clear structure with headings and bullet points
- Include data, examples, and case studies
- Add relevant images, charts, and diagrams
- Cite sources and provide external links where appropriate
- End with a call-to-action or invitation for further discussion

**Content Types That Perform Well:**
- Detailed how-to guides
- Industry trend analysis with data
- Comparison articles (tools, approaches, vendors)
- Expert explanations of complex topics
- Personal experience and case studies

### Strategy 3: Publish Zhihu Articles

Beyond answering questions, Zhihu allows users to publish standalone articles (文章) that function like blog posts.

**Article Strategy:**
- Publish 2-4 articles per month
- Write in-depth, original content (2,000-5,000 words)
- Cover topics that establish thought leadership
- Repurpose from your WeChat content (adapted for Zhihu's style)
- Include original research, data, and insights

### Strategy 4: Build Following and Engagement

**Growth Tactics:**
- Consistently provide high-quality answers
- Engage with comments on your answers
- Follow and interact with industry influencers
- Participate in relevant Zhihu topics (话题)
- Collaborate with other experts on joint content
- Share your Zhihu content on other platforms

### Strategy 5: Zhihu Live and Paid Content

For established accounts, Zhihu offers monetization options:
- **Zhihu Live (知乎Live):** Live streaming sessions on professional topics
- **Paid Consultations:** One-on-one paid consulting sessions
- **Paid Columns (付费专栏):** Subscription-based content series
- **Books (电子书):** Publish e-books on Zhihu's platform

These not only generate revenue but also strengthen authority signals.

## Integration: Baike + Zhihu + Your Website

The most effective authority building combines all three platforms:

1. **Baike** provides the foundational facts about your brand
2. **Zhihu** demonstrates your expertise and thought leadership
3. **Your Website** provides detailed product information and conversion paths

Cross-link between platforms where appropriate:
- Reference your Baike entry in Zhihu answers for credibility
- Link to Zhihu answers from your website for social proof
- Mention your Zhihu presence in Baike (where permitted)

## Measuring Authority Building Success

- **Baike Entry Quality:** Completeness, accuracy, and citation count
- **Zhihu Metrics:** Follower count, answer views, upvotes, and shares
- **AI Citation Rate:** How often your brand is cited by Chinese AI platforms
- **Referral Traffic:** Website visits from Baike and Zhihu
- **Brand Search Volume:** Growth in direct brand searches on Baidu
